Structural design of specific and industrial structures

The focus of structural design by RevoMann GmbH is on structures, where the challenges arise due to complexity of the corresponding analytical and numerical theories and specificness of the structure itself. These, more commonly, include combined form of shell, plate and beam structures.

In this regard, application of enhanced methods, such as finite element method, should be carried out carefully. This is only possible by under- standing different aspects of both modelling and structural engineering. Our main focus in design of industrial and specific structures are: different types of containers, industrial chimneys, platforms, pressure vessels, silos, spiral staircase, tanks as well as different types of foundations.

Fire safety and fire design.

Successful fire safety design requires an understanding of a wide range of issues and components, and the interactions between them such as, fire source, smoke movement, heat transfer to the building structure, detection, human behavior and toxicity.

We mainly focus on structural fire design of structures; from thermal analysis to evaluate structural performance using different design methods. These normally include cases that are specific: either not covered by codes or the results via simplified methods are not, economically, profitable. We also coordinate and collaborate in fire safety and design projects as well as material tests at elevated temperatures in collaboration with our partners from practice and academia in the following subjects:

  • - Tests for materials exposed to elevated temperatures;
  • - Tests for members exposed to elevated temperatures;
  • - Passive and active fire design;
  • - Fire and smoke development simulation.

Automation and IT solutions for civil engineering.

We provide solutions based on the needs of our clients in the practice. As an example, the main aim to automate structural calculations is to pro- vide simplified user interfaces for every day use of design and check/con- trol engineers.

Further, automation of work flows as well as other related IT solutions for companies with main focus on architectural and structural design as well as material production or verifications are main scope of our work.

Specific topics in structural engineering.

There are still number of specific topics that irrespective of use of numerical methods, challenge engineers in a high level. Some of related topics that have been the focus of RevoMann GmbH are:

  • - Nonlinear crack development of concrete;
  • - Nonlinear analysis of reinforced concrete foundations with piles or anchors;
  • - Nonlinear analysis of reinforced concrete structures at elevated temperatures above 200°C;
  • - Eigenfrequency analysis and response of structures;
  • - Seismic analysis of asymmetric reinforced concrete structures using 3D-models;
  • - Seismic analysis of steel structures using 3D-models;
  • - Buckling analysis of plate and shell structures using FE;
  • - Buckling analysis of steel cross-sections at elevated temperatures;
  • - Connection design;
  • - ...

Research.

We conduct, collaborate and coordinate research projects with our partners from practice and academia. These cover; - Structural and behavioral performance of structure, member, or interac- tion between two or multiple structures; - Investigation and comparison on behavior of industrial structures such as chimneys, cooling towers, silos, standing pressure vessels and underground tanks; - Fire design: from material and member fire tests to numerical investiga- tion and implementation into practice.
